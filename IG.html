Instagram Spots

Instagram is very popular these days, and to thrive on it you need aesthetic photos! Here are some locations that you can use as backgrounds!

1. The Pink Wall

The Pink Wall is next to the Paul Smith Store. It has a rainbow wall next to it too!

The Pink Wall is located at 8221 Melrose Ave, Los Angeles, CA

2. The Lamps at LACMA

The LACMA Lamps are made from 220 different lamps from the 1920s and 1930s! How cool!

The LACMA Lamps are located at LACMA Musemum!

3. Angel Wings Project

The Angel Wings Project was made to remind humans that we are the angels of this Earth. With multiple locates around the world, it is one of LA's most popular attraction.

These Wings can be found at 8401 W 3rd St, Los Angeles, CA 90048

5. The Broad Lights Museum

One of LA's most famous landmarks, The Broad's contemporary art museum has a number of rooms with muliple themes... From the room of light to the room of rain there is such a vast array of modern and post modern art! It's also free :)

These walls can be found here!
