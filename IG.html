Instagram Spots

Instagram is a very popular these days, and to thrive on it you need aesthetic photos! Here are some locations in which you can use as backgrounds!

1. The Pink Wall

The Pink Wall is next to the Paul Smith Store. It has a rainbow wall next to it too!

The Pink Wall is located at 8221 Melrose Ave, Los Angeles, CA

2. The Lamps at LACMA

The LACMA Lamps are made from 220 different lamps from the 1920s and 1930s!

The LACMA Lamps are located at LACMA Musemum!
