Cafes

1. Blue Butterfly Cafe

The Blue Butterfly Cafe is a small local cafe that serves tea, espresso, and smoothies. Great for a simple breakfast or lunch! It is very inexpensive and enjoyable!

Blue Butterfly Cafe is located in El Segundo, CA near LAX. 351 Main St, El Segundo, CA 90245. You can contact them at 1(310)640-7687.

2. The Butcher, The Baker, The Cappuccino Maker

The Butcher, The Baker, The Cappuccino Maker is a high-end bakery serving delicious pastries and perfect for brunch. They are one of the most reasonably priced cafes in LA and can be contacted at 1(310) 640-7687.

This cafe is located on Sunset Blvd! 8653 Sunset Blvd, West Hollywood, CA 90069

3. KC's Crepes Cafe

KC's Crepes offers both sweet and savory crepes coming in decorative cones. They offer a great variety of teas and coffee.

KC's Crepes is located in Westchester! 8320 Lincoln Blvd #101B, Los Angeles, CA 90045. They can be contacted at 1(310)348-9697.

4. Alfred

Known as a hip coffee shop, Alfred is a great place for a nice cold brew and even for getting work done!
