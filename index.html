About Us

We are part of the Kode With Klossy program, which hosts coding summer camps for girls ages 13-18 and fosters a national community furthering opportunities for girls in tech. Kode With Klossy empowers girls to learn to code and become leaders in tech. This is our final group project, and we hope you find this useful! :)

Our Mission

Our goal for this site is to give people a feel for different locations in LA. From coffee shops to the the best Instagram spots, we have found the most popular LA locations and displayed them here! We aim to give everyone a (reasonably priced) diverse taste of LA.
